Term definitions grouped by letter

A

Account verification (KYC)
The statutory identity check every licensed operator must complete before allowing play or paying out winnings.
Active bonus
A bonus whose wagering requirement has not yet been completed; its balance is held separately from withdrawable cash.

B

Bankroll
The money a player has set aside exclusively for play — a budgeting tool, not an investment fund.
Bonus wagering (rollover)
The total stakes required before a bonus balance converts to withdrawable cash, expressed as a multiple of the bonus.

C

Cashback
A partial return of net losses over a period, credited under published conditions; at licensed operators always opt-in.
Certified RNG
A random number generator tested by an accredited laboratory and filed with the supervisory authority before deployment.
