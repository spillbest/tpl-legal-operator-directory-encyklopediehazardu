Promo codes for existing players

Licensed operators may address registered players with opt-in codes. Typical families:

  • Reload codes for a stated deposit window, always opt-in
  • Loyalty and anniversary codes tied to the player account
  • Seasonal codes around major sporting or lottery events

Existing-player codes follow the same disclosure rules as welcome offers; an operator may never condition a withdrawal on accepting one.

What is a casino promo code and how does it work

A promo code is a short text string entered during registration or in the cashier that links a specific, pre-published offer to your account. At licensed operators the code never changes the rules of the underlying games — it only selects which bonus terms apply.

  • Enter the code exactly as published, before completing the deposit
  • The full bonus terms must be displayed before you confirm opt-in
  • A code can always be declined; playing without a bonus is a sound choice

Frequently asked questions

1️⃣Do I have to pay anything to use a free promo code?
No. No-deposit codes require only registration and identity verification. Withdrawal of any winnings is subject to the stated wagering requirement and cap.
2️⃣Why did my code not work?
Most failures are expired validity, a previously claimed welcome offer, or entry after the deposit was confirmed. Check the validity date in our table and the operator's terms.
3️⃣Are promo codes legal in the Czech market?
Yes, when published by an operator holding a valid national license and presented with complete conditions. Codes from unlicensed sites are not enforceable and we never list them.
