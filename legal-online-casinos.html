Safety and national legislation – what a licensed casino must meet

To obtain and keep a national license, an operator must satisfy statutory requirements covering capital adequacy, certified game software, data protection and player-protection tooling. The key obligations:

  • Registered office or branch in the licensing jurisdiction and audited accounts
  • Certified RNG and published RTP for every game offered
  • Mandatory identity and age verification before first play
  • Connection to the national self-exclusion register
  • Deposit, loss and time limits offered to every player
  • Segregated player funds and guaranteed payout of balances

What a domestic license guarantees players

Playing with a domestically licensed operator gives players enforceable rights that offshore sites do not provide:

  • Disputes can be escalated to the national supervisory authority free of charge
  • Player balances are protected even if the operator becomes insolvent
  • Advertising and bonus terms are subject to statutory fairness rules
  • Winnings are paid under domestic law and tax rules are transparent
  • Self-exclusion applies across all licensed operators at once
Domestic license vs other jurisdictions

Licenses issued in other jurisdictions may be legitimate there, but they do not grant local players the protections above. A comparison of licensing regimes is covered in Legislation and History.

Who supervises licensed online casinos

The national supervisory authority issues licenses, maintains the public register, audits operators and enforces sanctions. Its remit includes blocking unlicensed websites and payment flows, reviewing game certifications and handling player complaints.

Frequently asked questions

1️⃣How do I know an online casino is legal here?
Check the operating company in the supervisory authority's official register. Every operator in our table above holds a valid national license; the license date and company name match the register.
2️⃣What happens if I play at an unlicensed casino?
You lose all statutory protections: no complaint route, no guaranteed payouts, no cross-operator self-exclusion. The site may also be blocked at any time, complicating access to your balance.
3️⃣How often is this whitelist updated?
We verify the table against the official register every month and immediately after the regulator publishes a new decision. Each check is recorded in the maintenance note at the top of the page.
4️⃣Are bonuses at licensed casinos different from offshore offers?
Yes. Statutory rules cap aggressive bonus mechanics and require transparent wagering terms. Offers may look smaller but their conditions are enforceable.
5️⃣Does a license guarantee I will win?
No. A license guarantees fair, certified games and enforceable player rights — never outcomes. All casino games carry a house edge; play only with money you can afford to spend on entertainment.
